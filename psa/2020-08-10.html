UGWA now has the proper alternate schedule on the first day of school, in addition to a few other changes.

  • Wednesday SELF has been replaced with Gunn Together, where you meet with your teachers from Periods 1 through 7 (they cycle through each period every week). Currently, the first week's Gunn Together is with Period 5, then the second week and onwards cycles starting from Period 1, but I might have guessed wrong.
  • SELF has taken over the school, so the option to hide SELF has been removed.
  • Period 8 has been added to the schedule. You can hide it in the Options. Editing period H times has been removed.

Side note: It's possible the school no longer uses their Google Calendar to post alternate schedules ;-;.
